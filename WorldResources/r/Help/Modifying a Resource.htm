Modifying a Resource

By selecting to edit resources, you will be presented with a window that is much like the image below. All fields that are marked with * must be filled or selected, if you want to make a modification. If you do make a modification or delete a resource, changes will be present in the table, and in the list of resources in Main Window
You can search for your resources by their ID, and filter them by their type. Every change in filter and search will be registered, and you will see all resources as you select the filter or type in the search box.
All other fields can be empty or not selected. If you make a wrong entry, you will be forced to change it and will be promped with a message, like on the image below.
