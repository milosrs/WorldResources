Adding a New Tag

By selecting to add a new tag, you will be presented with a window that is much like the image below. All fields that are marked with * must be filled or selected.
You have to select an color for your tag to distinguish them. After adding them into you project, they will be shown as a colored box, with its description next to it. Resources may have none or many tags.
All other fields can be empty or not selected. If you make a wrong entry, you will be forced to change it and will be promped with a message, like on the image below.
